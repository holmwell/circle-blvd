Where your donation goes

Circle Blvd is being created by Secret Project, a software company based in Corvallis, Oregon, focused on the needs of small, volunteer-run organizations.

Secret Project is led by Phil Manijak, a co-founder of the Corvallis Swing Dance Society and journeyman computer programmer. Phil has been leading small teams in volunteer organizations for over six years, and has been programming in the software industry for ten.

Your donation will help us keep the lights on until word gets out about our great website. We rent our computers from Amazon; they are the recipient of all our operational fees. A small processing fee will go to Stripe.

Our work would not be possible without the support of other free software projects, including Apache CouchDB, Node.js, Bootstrap, and AngularJS.
